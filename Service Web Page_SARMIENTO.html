Adopt a friend!

“feline” is a cat shelter that aims to provide a safe and comfortable environment for stray cats. All adoptable cats will be spayed/neutered once entering the shelter. These fur babies have lived a tough life on the streets on their own so “feline” intends to make sure that they are adopted by loving humans and will be staying with them in their forever homes.
